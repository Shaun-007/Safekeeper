HITMAN

ROHIT SHARMA

About

Rohit Sharma, one of India’s most celebrated cricketers, is renowned for his elegant batting style, calm leadership, and record-breaking achievements. Known as the “Hitman,” he holds multiple world records, including the highest individual score in ODI cricket. As a captain and senior player, Rohit has inspired millions with his composed approach, strategic thinking, and ability to perform under pressure. His journey from a young talent in Mumbai to an international cricket icon continues to motivate fans around the world.

Quick Facts

  • Full Name: Rohit Gurunath Sharma
  • Born: April 30, 1987, in Nagpur, Maharashtra, India
  • Role: Batsman, occasional Right-arm Offbreak bowler
  • Batting Style: Right-handed
  • Teams: India National Team, Mumbai Indians (IPL)
  • ODI Debut: June 23, 2007, vs Ireland
  • Test Debut: November 6, 2013, vs West Indies
  • T20I Debut: September 19, 2007, vs England
  • Nickname: Hitman
  • Records: Highest individual score in ODI cricket (264 runs), 3 double centuries in ODIs
